!add allows me to add up all of your values followed by the command. 
For example: !add 5 5 --> 10.0 

!divide allows me to divide all of your values followed by the command (don't add zero after your first number!)
For example: !divide 5 5 --> 1.0 

!multiply allows me to multiply all of your values followed by the command. 
For example: !multiply 5 5 --> 25.0 

!minus allows me to subtract all of your values followed by the command. 
For example: !minus 5 5 --> 0.0
